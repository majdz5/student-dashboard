Student Dashboard Help

1. Overview

Manage Your Courses

Keep track of all your enrolled courses in one place. View course details, important notes, and old sessions. This makes it easier to stay on top of your coursework and stay organized throughout the semester.

Pomodoro Timer for Focused Study

Take advantage of the built-in Pomodoro Timer, a productivity technique that helps you manage study sessions and breaks. By working in focused intervals and taking regular short breaks, you can improve concentration, boost productivity, and avoid burnout.

Track Your Assignments

Stay on top of all your assignments and deadlines. The dashboard provides a to-do list to prioritize tasks efficiently, helping you manage your time between your assignments and projects.

Calendar Management

Sync your class schedules, exam dates, deadlines, and personal events in one place with the calendar feature. This helps you plan ahead and avoid missing any important events.

University News and Events

Stay informed about the latest university news, announcements, and events. This ensures you never miss out on anything important.

In summary, the Student Dashboard is an all-in-one platform designed to help you manage your academic life, improve your productivity, and stay motivated. With features that support study organization, task management, time management, and university updates, the dashboard makes your academic journey smoother and more productive.

2. How to Navigate the Dashboard

To navigate, use the sidebar to access different sections such as Dashboard, Courses, News, and Calendar.
