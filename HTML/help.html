Overview

Dashboard

The Dashbord is your quick access section to various tools you might need as a student. It includes a Todo-List for your daily task management, a Pomodoro Timer for time management, a motivational widget to keep you inspired, a small reminder of your latest assignments, and a progress tracker to track your studies!

Courses

The Courses page includes all your courses, seperated into folders, and each folder split into: Material, Sessions, and Student notes. They all link to a Google drive folder that is open access for everyone to add to. This page also includes all our latest assignements(this is only meant as a reminder)

Calendar

The Calendar page includes a weekly timeline of our semester courses. (Will be more interactive in the future, as for now it is only an exported widget.)

About Us

The idea behind this project was to make the LU students' life easier. This website is meant to have (almost) everything you need. It's supposed to be a place that brings everythig together and makes it easy-access. With that said, we plan on expanding this more in the future and including more useful features. If you have any idea or suggestion for a feature, you're more than welcome to submit it in the form below. If you noticed any bugs or strange behavior, we would also love to hear about it. Thank you!
